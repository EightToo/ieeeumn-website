## Tech Committee

The Tech Committee is a place for members who want to build and learn by doing.
Each semester, teams work on a shared technical project that’s intentionally
open-ended, giving plenty of room for creativity and experimentation.

Members work in small teams and are supported with resources, materials, and
guidance throughout the build process. No prior experience required--just
curiosity and a willingness to participate.

This semester’s project focuses on building an USB HID device, such as a
keyboard, controller, or other custom input device that connects to a computer.
Teams choose their own ideas and approach, whether that’s something practical or
more exploratory. Check out past and ongoing projects [here](/projects)!

## Requirements

**Lower Division** (first and second year students):

- Attend 1 general meeting
- Attend 1 committee meeting
- Attend 1 IEEE event

**Upper Division** (third year students and up):

- Fulfill all lower division requirements
- Attend 1 ROE event
